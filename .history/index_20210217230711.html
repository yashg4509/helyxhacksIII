About

Helyx Hacks III is the one of the largest online biotech-based hackathons where high school students around the world innovate impactful software projects relating to the secret theme released at the opening ceremony. Students team up in groups of 2 to 4 or work alone to build an app, game, program, or website. Students will have 7 days, from April 2nd, 2021 at 10 AM PST to April 9th, 2021 at 4PM PST, to create an amazing product and submit it to compete for thousands of dollars of prizes. The hackathon is a good way to show off your skills and learn something new!

Rules
1. Be nice to everyone
We want to make sure everyone feels welcome and safe to our hackathon and will take action against any violation of this.
2. Don't feel afraid to ask questions.
We want to make sure that everyone learns and benefits from this hackathon. The best way to do so is taking that step and asking our many active mentors on our Discord. We are happy to help!
3. Please remember that there will be NO monetary prizes for international students outside of the United States.
Unfortunately, due to the difference in payments and net of logistics to transfer money/physical prizes to other countries, it has been a legal and financial hassle that we will not be able to overcome. However, we will try to provide software prizes to encourage those internationally who want to compete. Also, remember that it isn't only about prizes!
4. Remember to submit through Devpost with the appropriate information required.
Please remember to submit your work and sign up for the Nucleotide Hackathon on Devpost here.

FAQs
Is this hackathon an online event or offline?
Hack on hills is offline event and as a participant you need to be present at NIT Hamirpur campus one or two day before the event at least.
Do this hackathon has any participation fees for students?
No. The hackathon is free of cost for any student from any college all over India.
What are all the restrictions on Team formations for the event?
A team can have at most 4 members and minimum of 2 members.
What should I do if I do not have any team yet?
Just fill out the application form and according to skills you have filled out, we will match you with the best team possible during Pre-Hackathon meeting.
What are the Perks and Benefits of Participating in this Hackathon?
Hackathon Provide hands on experience with the experts from different field working on the Projects like Blockchain, AI, Machine Learning, etc. This will help to give a head start if any one want to make project on the field. Along with that the participant will be provided with cool swags, stickers and refreshments to keep them up.
What are the Accommodation Facility for the Participant from Outside the College?
Participant will be provided with Accommodation and Food in the Guest House of the College during the Hackathon Period.
How to reach NIT Hamirpur for the event?
The nearest airport is Dharamshala (Gaggal, district Kangra). You can easily find flights from Delhi airport to Gaggal airport. From Gaggal bus stand, you have to take bus or private taxi to Hamirpur. Otherwise, direct buses to Hamirpur is also available from Delhi ISBT, from Chandigarh and from other places.
